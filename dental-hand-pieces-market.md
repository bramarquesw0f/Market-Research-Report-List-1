<p><p>The dental handpiece market comprises three main types: air-driven, electric, and hybrid dental pieces. Air-driven dental pieces utilize compressed air for rotation and are widely used for their reliability and cost-effectiveness. Electric dental pieces offer consistent speed and torque, enhancing precision in procedures while reducing hand fatigue. Hybrid dental pieces combine features of both air-driven and electric types, providing versatility and improved performance. Each type caters to different preferences and procedural needs in modern dentistry.</p></p>
