<p><p>Aluminium alloys die casting parts are produced through various methods, primarily vacuum die casting and pressure die casting. Vacuum die casting removes air from the mold, resulting in higher quality parts with fewer defects, making it ideal for complex shapes. Pressure die casting, on the other hand, involves injecting molten aluminium into molds under high pressure, enabling rapid production of precise and durable components, suitable for high-volume manufacturing scenarios. Both methods cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
