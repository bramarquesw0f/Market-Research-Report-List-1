<p><ul><li>Passive Dressing</li><li>Bioactive Dressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin wound dressings market features various types, including passive and bioactive dressings. Passive dressings, such as gauze or adhesive films, primarily provide a protective barrier to prevent infection and promote a moist healing environment without active therapeutic properties. Conversely, bioactive dressings contain bioactive agents that actively facilitate healing through properties such as antimicrobial activity, moisture retention, or enhanced cellular response. Together, these dressings cater to diverse wound care needs, improving patient outcomes and expediting recovery.</p></p>
