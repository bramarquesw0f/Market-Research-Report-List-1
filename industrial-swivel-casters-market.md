<p><p>The industrial swivel casters market features various types designed for different applications. Hollow kingpin casters have a hollow axle construction, allowing for weight distribution and smoother operation. Plate casters are designed with a flat mounting surface for easy attachment to various equipment, ensuring versatile mobility in industrial settings.</p></p>
