<p><p>Corner or edge bonding pastes are specialized adhesives used in electronic packaging and assembly. The market is divided into two main types: reworkable and non-reworkable pastes. Reworkable bonding pastes allow for the easy removal and replacement of components during repairs or upgrades, providing flexibility in manufacturing processes. In contrast, non-reworkable pastes create permanent bonds, offering high durability and reliability but limiting the ability to modify or repair the assembly. Both types cater to different applications based on performance and design requirements.</p></p>
