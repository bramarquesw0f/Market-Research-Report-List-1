<p><p>The Post CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) Cleaning Chemicals market is divided into two primary categories: acidic and alkaline materials. Acidic materials, such as sulfuric acid and nitric acid, are used to effectively remove metal contaminants and organic residues from wafer surfaces. In contrast, alkaline materials, including ammonium hydroxide and potassium hydroxide, are employed for overall cleaning and to neutralize acidic residues. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the cleanliness and integrity of semiconductor surfaces post-CMP processing.</p></p>
