<p><p>The cystic fibrosis therapeutics market includes several key types. Pancreatic enzyme supplements help improve digestion by replacing enzymes that the pancreas fails to produce. Mucolytics thin mucus in the lungs, facilitating easier clearance and reducing infections. Bronchodilators relax airway muscles, enhancing airflow and alleviating breathing difficulties. CFTR modulators target the underlying defect in the CFTR protein, improving its function and overall disease management. Together, these therapies address the multifaceted challenges of cystic fibrosis, improving patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
