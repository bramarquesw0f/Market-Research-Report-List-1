<p><p>The industrial silicone fluid market comprises two main types: straight silicone fluid and modified silicone fluid. Straight silicone fluid, characterized by its stable viscosity and thermal resistance, is widely used in applications like lubricants and coatings. Modified silicone fluid, on the other hand, incorporates additives to enhance properties such as adherence, wettability, or chemical resistance, making it suitable for specialized applications in industries like automotive, textiles, and construction. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs by offering unique performance characteristics.</p></p>
