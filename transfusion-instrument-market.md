<p><strong>The Transfusion Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Infusion Pump</li><li>Gravity-delivered Infusion Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transfusion instrument market includes two main types of infusion pumps: volumetric infusion pumps and gravity-delivered infusion pumps. Volumetric infusion pumps accurately control the flow rate and volume of fluids administered, making them suitable for precise medication delivery. In contrast, gravity-delivered infusion pumps rely on gravitational force to administer fluids, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution, though with less control over flow rates. Both types are essential for safe and effective blood transfusions and fluid administration in medical settings.</p></p>
