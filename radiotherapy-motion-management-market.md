<p><ul><li>Spirometric Motion Management</li><li>Intrafraction Radiotherapy Motion Management</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiotherapy motion management encompasses techniques to maintain precise targeting of tumors during treatment. Spirometric motion management utilizes real-time monitoring of respiratory patterns to adjust radiation delivery, minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissues. Intrafraction radiotherapy motion management focuses on monitoring and compensating for patient movement during each treatment session, ensuring the radiation beam remains accurately aligned. Other methods may include image-guided techniques or mechanical solutions that enhance stability and precision during radiotherapy, ultimately improving treatment efficacy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
