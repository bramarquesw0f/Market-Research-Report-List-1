<p><p>The Remote Keyless Systems market is primarily categorized into Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) and Active Keyless Entry (AKE) systems. PKE systems allow users to unlock and start vehicles without physically pressing a button, utilizing sensors that detect proximity. In contrast, AKE systems require users to press a button on the key fob to unlock or start the vehicle. Both types enhance convenience and security, driving their adoption in modern automotive applications, with growing demand for advanced technology integration.</p></p>
