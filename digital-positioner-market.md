<p><p>The Digital Positioner Market can be categorized into Single Acting and Double Acting Positioners. Single Acting Positioners control devices that use pressure from a single direction, mainly for applications requiring minimal control efforts. Conversely, Double Acting Positioners utilize pressure from both sides, enabling more precise control in applications with varying loads and demands. These market segments cater to diverse industries, including oil and gas, water management, and manufacturing, each selecting positioners based on the specific fluid control needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
