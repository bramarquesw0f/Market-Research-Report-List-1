<p><p>The industrial cryogenic valves market includes various types designed for handling extremely low temperatures. Ball valves provide reliable sealing and quick shut-off, making them ideal for systems requiring tight control. Gate valves offer minimal pressure drop and are suited for on/off applications in larger pipelines. Butterfly valves are lightweight with a compact design, suitable for regulating flow in extensive systems. Other valve types may include check valves and pressure relief valves, each catering to specific operational needs within cryogenic applications.</p></p>
